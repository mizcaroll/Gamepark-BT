Attractions

Nairobi Gamepark is the only wildlife park within the city, which features the endangered Black Rhinoceros.

Boasting of its diverse birdlife; the park has over 400 bird species. At least 20 of which are seasonal European migrants. The park is also home to Large predators including the lion, leopard, hyena and cheetah as well as aggregations of large herbivores- elephant, buffalo, giraffe, zebra and wildebeast.

There are defined walking trails next to hippo pools ideal for hikes, nature walks & adventurous excursions. There are plently spacious & accommodating venues for corporate and special events as well as picnic sites for camp lovers.

Home to over 100 mammal species, four of the Big Five (lion, Buffalo, leopard and rhino); the park's unbeaten attraction is its diverse wildlife and scenery.
